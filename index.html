Shipping Tips & Information

How to Pack Safely

  • Use sturdy boxes with proper cushioning material
  • Wrap fragile items individually in bubble wrap
  • Fill empty spaces to prevent items from shifting
  • Seal boxes with strong packing tape
  • Label clearly with recipient and sender information
  • Include packing list inside the box

Best Gifts from USA to Europe

  • American candy and snacks (Reese's, Pop-Tarts)
  • Brand clothing and shoes (Nike, Levi's)
  • Electronics and gadgets
  • Vitamins and supplements
  • Local crafts and souvenirs
  • Books and magazines in English
  • American coffee and specialty foods

Customs & Tax Information

  • EU: Gifts under €45 are duty-free
  • Commercial goods: 19-27% VAT applies
  • Always declare true value on customs forms
  • Keep receipts for all items shipped
  • Some items may require special permits
  • Prohibited items: liquids, batteries, food products
