How to Pack Your Items

✅ What You CAN Send

  • Electronics (phones, laptops, tablets)
  • Clothing and shoes
  • Books and documents
  • Household items
  • Cosmetics (non-liquid)
  • Supplements and vitamins

❌ What You CANNOT Send

  • Liquids over 100ml
  • Batteries (lithium)
  • Weapons and ammunition
  • Perishable foods
  • Cash and valuables
  • Hazardous materials

💡 Packing Tips

  • Use sturdy boxes with padding
  • Wrap fragile items individually
  • Fill empty spaces to prevent movement
  • Label boxes clearly
  • Keep weight under 30kg per box
  • Include packing list inside

Helpful Articles & FAQ

📏 How Volumetric Weight Works

Volumetric weight is calculated by multiplying length × width × height and dividing by a dimensional factor (139 for sea, 169 for air). The higher of actual or volumetric weight determines shipping cost.

💰 How to Save on Shipping

Consolidate packages, choose sea freight for non-urgent items, pack efficiently to minimize dimensions, and consider our purchase consolidation service to combine multiple orders.

⏰ Delivery Timeframes

Air freight: 5-10 business days. Sea freight: 3-6 weeks. Express services available. Times may vary based on customs clearance and final destination.

📦 Package Tracking

We provide tracking numbers for all shipments. You can monitor your package from our Florida facility to your doorstep in Europe through our partner network.
