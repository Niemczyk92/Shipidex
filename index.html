How to Pack

Guidelines for proper packaging and prohibited items

Recommended Packing

  • Use sturdy, double-wall corrugated cardboard boxes
  • Wrap items individually with bubble wrap
  • Use adequate cushioning material (foam, packing peanuts)
  • Seal boxes with strong packing tape
  • Label boxes clearly with contents and handling instructions
  • Keep weight under 30kg per box for easy handling

Prohibited Items

  • Weapons and ammunition
  • Flammable liquids and gases
  • Toxic and hazardous materials
  • Perishable food items
  • Plants and seeds without proper documentation
  • Counterfeit goods and illegal items
  • Batteries without proper packaging

Import Taxes & Duties

Important information about customs procedures and fees

When shipping from USA to Europe, your goods may be subject to import taxes, customs duties, and other fees. These are determined by the destination country based on the type of goods, their value, and country of origin.

Key Information by Country:

Country Tax-Free Limit VAT Rate Customs Duty
Czech Republic 150 EUR 21% 0-17% based on product type
Poland 150 EUR 23% 0-20% based on product type
Slovakia 150 EUR 20% 0-17% based on product type
Hungary 150 EUR 27% 0-17% based on product type

Note: The recipient is typically responsible for paying all import taxes and duties. Rates may change, so always check with local customs for the most current information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shipping services

How long does shipping from USA to Europe take? +

Sea freight typically takes 4-6 weeks, while air freight takes 1-2 weeks. These times include customs clearance and final delivery to your address.

How is volumetric weight calculated? +

Volumetric weight is calculated by multiplying the length, width, and height of your package (in cm) and dividing by a volumetric factor. For sea freight we use /139, for air freight we use /169.

Do you provide packaging materials? +

Yes, we can provide packaging materials for an additional fee. Alternatively, you can use your own packaging as long as it meets our requirements.

Can I track my shipment? +

Yes, we provide tracking numbers for all shipments. You can track your package through our website or via email updates.

What happens if my package gets damaged? +

We offer insurance options for all shipments. In case of damage, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage and packaging for us to process your claim.
